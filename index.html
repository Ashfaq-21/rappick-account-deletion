Account Deletion Policy

Last updated: 21 January 2026

At Rappick, we respect your right to control your personal data. This policy explains the process for requesting account deletion, what happens to your data, and our retention practices.

1. How Users Request Account Deletion

Users can request account deletion by contacting our support team via email at support@rappick.com.

Please include your registered email address and a brief reason for deletion. Requests will be processed within 30 days after verification.

2. What Data is Deleted

  • Personal information (name, email, phone number, profile details)
  • Order history and transaction records
  • Location data and delivery preferences
  • Uploaded documents or images associated with the account

3. What Data (if any) is Retained

  • Anonymized data for analytics and service improvement
  • Data required for legal compliance (tax records, dispute resolution)
  • Backup copies during the retention period

4. Retention Period

  • Deleted data is removed from active systems within 30 days
  • Legally required data may be retained for up to 7 years
  • Backup data may be retained for up to 1 year

5. Additional Notes

Account deletion is irreversible. You will lose access to all services and data. If you have outstanding orders or payments, deletion may be delayed until resolution.

For questions, contact support@rappick.com.
